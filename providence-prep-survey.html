About the Program

What is Create Space?

Create Space is a 10-week program that blends hands-on creative sessions — think watercolor, sourdough, embroidery, candle making — with guided self-discovery, all inside a small, intimate cohort.

It's designed around space over productivity. No planning. No performing. No agenda to check off. You simply show up — and somewhere along the way, you leave with clearer self-awareness, deep new friendships, and often a hobby you didn't expect to love.

Sessions will take place right here at Providence Prep, so there's no extra drive across town. Just walk in, settle in, and breathe.
